Why I'm Building a Fitness Tracker from Scratch

Posted on May 19, 2025

I’ve always been obsessed with progress tracking. Lifting weights, logging sets and reps, tracking calories—these are part of my daily life. But the existing apps are either too bloated or too basic. So I’m setting out to build my own: a minimalist, data-backed fitness platform that adjusts to your goals over time.

This blog will document the process from idea to code, with a little bit of science, personal progress, and tech frustration along the way.
